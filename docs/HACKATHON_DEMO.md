# Hackathon Demo Script

1. Sign in with Google.
2. Complete relocation onboarding.
3. Enter workplace, salary, budget, and preferences.
4. Ask: `Help me relocate near Electronic City, Bengaluru, within INR 18,000 rent.`
5. Show the agent timeline and live source calls.
6. Display geocoding, nearby emergency facilities, route, weather, and source URLs.
7. Compare relocation scenarios in the budget planner.
8. Open verified owner-submitted accommodation.
9. Toggle Evidence Mode.
10. Save and share the plan.
11. Open the emergency offline card.
12. Finish on the Source Reliability dashboard.

Closing line:

> CityMate AI is not a chatbot that invents city recommendations. It is an evidence-first multi-agent decision platform that combines live open data, verified community listings, transparent calculations, and source-grounded AI guidance.
